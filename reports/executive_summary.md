# Executive Summary Template

## Objective

Analyze online retail transaction data to identify revenue trends, customer segments, retention patterns, and churn risk.

## Key Findings

Fill this section after analysis.

Example structure:

1. Monthly revenue shows strong seasonality, with peak sales concentrated near holiday periods.
2. A small share of customers contributes a large portion of total revenue.
3. Customers with high monetary value but long purchase recency represent the most important reactivation opportunity.
4. Certain products and countries drive disproportionate sales performance.
5. Cancellation and return behavior should be monitored because it can distort gross revenue.

## Business Recommendations

1. Launch reactivation campaigns for high-value inactive customers.
2. Prioritize retention offers for loyal and champion customers.
3. Monitor high-return products and review product quality or descriptions.
4. Use country-level revenue and AOV patterns to guide marketing allocation.
5. Track cohort retention monthly to detect customer lifecycle issues earlier.
